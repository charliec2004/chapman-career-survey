## 1. How Chapman career services are structured (routing model)

**Hub-and-spoke.** A central office (the "Career House") serves everyone and runs the shared
online platforms. Most colleges also have a college-specific advisor/center for
discipline-aware guidance.

- **Default route:** Central Office of Career & Professional Development.
- **Preferred route when major is known:** the student's college-specific career office.
- **Scheduling platform** is **Handshake** for nearly everyone. Exceptions: **Law → Symplicity**,
  **Pharmacy → "CUSPark" [UNVERIFIED]**.
- Most online tools (VMock, CareerShift, Vault, GoinGlobal) are accessed **through Handshake**.
  **The Panther Network** is the exception (its own portal).

**Durable links to prefer in the app:** the canonical
`chapman.edu/campus-services/career-professional-development/...` resource pages and
`https://chapman.joinhandshake.com/login`. Deep Handshake article IDs are real but can change.

### Platform details

**Handshake** — Chapman's central career portal. Schedule advising appointments
(`/appointments/new`), search/apply for jobs & internships, register for events/workshops,
read resource articles. Access point for most other tools. Free to students (institutional).

**Chapman Career AI (VMock)** — "Reviewing platform powered by AI to instantaneously provide
real-time feedback on your resume, cover letter, LinkedIn and Mock Interview." Resume scoring,
cover-letter review, LinkedIn ("Aspire") optimization, and AI mock interviews with real-time
analysis. **Explicitly complimentary for Chapman students and alumni.**

**CareerShift** — Job-search organization + contact database. Find employer/alumni contacts,
aggregate postings (LinkedIn, Indeed, private company pages), organize with folders/notes.
Cost not printed on page (presumed free) **[UNVERIFIED-COST]**.

**Vault (Firsthand)** — Employer/industry rankings, ratings & reviews on thousands of employers
and hundreds of internship programs; exclusive job board + advice. **[UNVERIFIED-COST]**.

**GoinGlobal** — Global job market knowledge (~80,000 pages), international jobs/internships,
country-specific guides. **[UNVERIFIED-COST]**.

**The Panther Network** — Chapman-exclusive networking/mentorship; connect & message 80,000+
alumni; import LinkedIn profile. Own portal, log in with Chapman email. Free to students.

## 6. Branding (Chapman-inspired; do NOT reproduce official logo/seal)
Source of truth: https://brand.chapman.edu/

### Colors (official hex)
| Name | Hex | Tier | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Chapman Red** | `#A50034` | Primary | Signature color; headlines, accents, color blocks. Deep cardinal, not bright red. AA on white (~7.3:1). |
| **Panther Black** | `#231F20` | Primary | Logos + **body copy**. Warm near-black, not `#000`. |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Primary | Backgrounds, negative space. |
| **Grove** (green) | `#00966C` | Secondary | Headlines, subheads, lead-ins, quotes. |
| **Pacific** (teal) | `#009CA6` | Secondary | Sparing accent for intensity. |
| **Pillar** (warm grey) | `#6E6259` | Tertiary | Warm taupe-grey neutral. |
| **Sand** (tan) | `#DDCBA4` | Tertiary | Backgrounds/accents. **Not for text on white** (fails contrast). |

> Chapman's "grey" is warm **Pillar** `#6E6259`, not a cool neutral. Use a separate neutral grey
> ramp for UI scaffolding; treat Pillar as a warm accent neutral.

### Typography
- **Official:** Futura PT (headings, geometric sans), Futura PT Condensed (tight headlines),
  Minion Pro (serif body), Arial (official fallback). Futura PT & Minion are Adobe-licensed.
- **Free web substitutes (unofficial, for this build):**
  - Headings: `"Futura PT", "Jost", "Century Gothic", Arial, sans-serif` (Jost ≈ Futura geometry)
  - Body: `"Minion Pro", "Source Serif 4", Georgia, serif`

### Visual style
Geometric & architectural. Signature motif is the **window**; second shape is the **triangle**
(headline backdrops). Clean, precise, modern; strong color blocking; thin grey divider lines;
lots of white space with bold red accents. Photography uses shaped crops (window/triangle/
trapezoid). Net feel: contemporary and confident, not old-collegiate; minimal-leaning but
energetic, not austere.

### Voice & tagline
- **Tagline:** "Driven by Curiosity. Inspired by Chapman."
- **Voice:** bold, personal/authentic, confident, consistent.
- The dominant brand **symbol is the window, not the panther**. (Mascot: Pete the Panther.)
- Do **not** reproduce the official logo, window icon, or seal in this unofficial build —
  emulate palette, type, and geometric style instead.
